Animal Barn

Food & Supplies for Pemberton Pet Owners

Located in Pemberton and serving the surrounding areas of Squamish, Whistler and Goldbridge

Animal Barn has a wide selection of pet food and supplies. Our shelves include everything from canned cat food to animal fencing to dog leashes!

Besides residential pet products, we also sell a variety of farming and chicken coop supplies. We even offer horse supplements, feed and bridles for breeders and trainers.

Open every day of the week

Find What Your Pets Need at Animal Barn Since our start in 2000, Animal Barn has always been focused on customer satisfaction. We understand how important it is that your pets stay happy and healthy – we want to provide you with high quality, industry trusted pet food and animal supplies so you know you’re giving your pet the best.

With our affordable prices and helpful staff, you’re sure to find just what you need. Visit Animal Barn today to see our selection for yourself!
